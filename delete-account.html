Delete Your Account – TEXT App

If you want to delete your account and all associated data from the TEXT App, please follow the steps below:

📩 How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to:

textappsupport@gmail.com

Make sure to include your registered email ID or username used in the app.

🗑️ What Happens Next?

  • Your account will be permanently deleted.
  • All your data including messages and profile information will be removed.
  • This process cannot be undone.

⏱️ Processing Time

Your account deletion request will be processed within 24–48 hours.

⚠️ If we need more information to verify your identity, we may contact you before deleting your account.

📌 Important

Some data may be retained for a limited time if required for legal or security purposes.
